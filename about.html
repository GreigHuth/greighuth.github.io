BUT WHO ARE YOU?

As i said, i am Greig and this is my website. I do many things, but mostly i play video games and fiddle with computers. I've been in and out of a couple jobs since graduating from university and decided i really need to get myself one of those fancy BlogsTM that are so popular. My love of games has motivated me to pursue game design and development so the main purpose of this site is to have a portfolio i can whap out when i need to. I live in Scotland, i may also have been born here but my memory of that event is mysteriously hazy.

BUT WHAT DO YOU REALLY DO?

My current job is doing IT/System Admin/Random Things at smartR.ai. Its a few hours of contract work a week usually involving me fiddling with OCI containters or resetting passwords or uninstalling windows :).

I went to the University Of Edinburgh for computer science and graduated in 2021. To put it simply i enjoy learning about how things work and my love of games naturally steered me to pursue computing as a profession. My degree gave me a good grasp of the fundamentals, and it has lead me to my next goal, game development. I've always been fascinated by video games. They represent a unique intersection of art, interactivity and technology that i find continually compelling and rewarding. Many games have inspired me but if i had to narrow it down to three it would be: Shadow Of The Colossus, Dark Souls, and The Legend Of Zelda Wind Waker.

Apart from gushing, i have actually doing things. As of writing im still in the process of developing a 2D jump-and-shoot game and ive also been learning about 3D Computer Graphics and 3D Modelling. I also occasionally do photography but im nothing special.
