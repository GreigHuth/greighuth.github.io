BUT WHO ARE YOU?

Ostensibly not a graphic designer

I am Greig, or Gurg, and this is my website. I studied Computer Science at The University of Edinburgh, and after being dissatisfied with almost every job Ive had, Ive decided to focus on being happy rather than being "successful". I am many things but mostly I am someone who likes video games and wants to make video games. I enjoy games from literally all gaming genres, but i mostly play: 3rd Person "find and fight" games, platformers', Non-Western RPGs and fighting games.

BUT WHAT DO YOU REALLY DO?

I complain a lot, but i try to make it worthwhile

In the past I've worked as: An admin assistant at a law firm, a kitchen porter in a busy and toxic high street restaurant, as the only technically competent individual in a growing e-commerce startup, and as a university tutor. My most recent job has been doing System Administration @ smartR.ai. This work has included an assortment of jobs ranging from: tech support, buying company hardware, containerizing and deploying essential company services and cleaning up messes.

As previously mentioned I want to make video games. Ive been slowly but surely developing my skills ranging from 3D modelling and modding to gameplay system and graphics programming and gained some experience with a few SDKs and engines (Pico-8, Playdate SDK, Unity) but its hard to maintain momentum. My progress is taking time but thats fine because im not in a rush. Making a good game in my opinion means involves playing, watching, analysing and experimenting with a lot of video games. I don't play them for fun, i try to understand why im having fun. I take time to think about why im enjoying what im doing right now, and often have more fun discussing the game then actually playing it. To that end, I love playing crap games, my growing PS1 collection is at least 60% pure rubbish. If it interests you, here's an incomplete list of every game ive played an hour of.
